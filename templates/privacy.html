Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter manually.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps to maintain account security.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers and is associated only with your account. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use minimal infrastructure services (such as hosting providers) to operate the app; these providers are contractually required to keep your data confidential and may not use it for any other purpose.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your expense data at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support page. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
